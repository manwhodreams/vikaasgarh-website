About VikaasGarh

Building the foundation for Chhattisgarh’s growth.

VikaasGarh is a sector-agnostic platform building Chhattisgarh's regional ecosystem through skill-based education, incubation, and acceleration.

VikaasGarh exists to build a sustainable ecosystem infrastructure for Chhattisgarh through long-term ecosystem development initiatives, continuous stakeholder engagement, and collaboration. It works to strengthen the ecosystem capabilities of the state while creating greater access to opportunities, resources, and support for its people at different stages of their journey.
